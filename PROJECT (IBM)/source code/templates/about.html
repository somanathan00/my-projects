About us

What we are doing

Talented people are getting Jobs

The company was incorporated on May 1, 1995 under the Companies Act, 1956 as job hunter(India) Private Limited and became a public limited company on April 27, 2006. Starting with a classified recruitment online business, jobfinder.com, Job hunter has grown and diversified rapidly, setting benchmarks as a pioneer for others to follow. Driven by innovation, creativity, an experienced and talented leadership team and a strong culture of entrepreneurship, today, it is India’s premier online classifieds company in recruitment, matrimony, real estate, education and related services.
